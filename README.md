## About FIX FCT

FIX FCT is a non-partisan civic engagement and community development initiative dedicated to transforming the Federal Capital Territory (FCT), Nigeria, through technology, responsible leadership, transparency, and active citizen participation.

The project provides an integrated **Website**, **Mobile Application**, and **Digital Civic Platform** that enables residents to identify community challenges, collaborate on practical solutions, monitor development projects, volunteer for community service, and engage constructively with government and stakeholders.

Our vision is simple:

> **Together we can Fix the FCT.**

---

# Vision

To build a prosperous, inclusive, secure, and technologically driven Federal Capital Territory where every citizen has the opportunity to contribute to sustainable development.

---

# Mission

To empower residents of the Federal Capital Territory through innovative digital platforms that promote civic participation, transparency, accountability, collaboration, and community-driven development.

---

# Objectives

- Promote active citizen participation
- Improve accountability in governance
- Encourage community development
- Support data-driven decision-making
- Increase transparency
